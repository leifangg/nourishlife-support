FAQ

Q: What is NourishMyself?

A: NourishMyself is a self-nurturing growth app that helps you build healthy habits through emotional journaling, focus training, and cognitive behavioral tools.

Q: Is my data safe?

A: Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or upload any personal information.

Q: Which devices are supported?

A: NourishMyself supports iPhone running iOS 17.0 or later.
